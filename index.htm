Oxford dictionary explain
'legacy'
as "a situation that exists now because of events, actions etc., that took place in the past".

About 30 years ago Kumily had few tourists and very few accommodation facilities. A visionary started a lodge named MUCKUMKAL TOURIST HOME in 1986 at the heart of Kumily town, the gateway of Thekkady. It grew with the suggestions & complaints of its respected customers !

Now it is 'muckumkal ROYAL NEST' a budget hotel to cater the needs of tourists and business people coming and passing this way. Repeat customers and their references are the backbone of this customer friendly hotel.

In short, A LEGACY ESTABLISHED IN 1986 ...!
